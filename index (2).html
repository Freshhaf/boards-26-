üìù During the Exam

  • First 15 minutes (Reading Time): Scan all questions, mark easy ones, plan your sequence
  • Start with easy questions: Build confidence, secure marks quickly
  • Time management: Don't spend more than allocated time per question

  • Diagrams: Label clearly, use pencil, keep them neat
  • Attempt everything: Even if unsure, write something relevant
  • Last 10 minutes: Review, check name/roll number on every sheet
